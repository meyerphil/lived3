Who are we?

LiveD3 is a band, software development team, and film studio.

We are twin brothers, who share a large passion in music, film, and programming. Both starting at 9 years old in playing music, and starting film freshmen year, still will continue pursuing our passion. Now 15 years old, we work together as one, outputting each and every piece of work as if it were our last, making it our best.

Our goal in our work is to bring joy and inspire others, which neither has failed.

In our films and other projects, creativity is key, no matter how crazy or childlike our ideas are. Our music tends to conduct a more mature side. We have put all of our natural creativity and work in our pieces, with each one representing our current skill and mind sets.

Run and Founded by the creative Phil Meyer(left). Co-Founded and Assisted by talented Dallas Meyer(right).
